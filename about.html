Introduction

Dr. Xu Gong is currently working as Associate Research Scientist at the Center for Brain Disorder and Cognitive Science, and College of Psychology, at the Shenzhen University, Shenzhen Key Laboratory of Affective and Social Cognitive Science.

She obtained her doctorate in Cognitive Neuroscience at the Center for Cognitive Neuroimaging, Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour at the Radboud University in the Netherlands and State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience and LearningBeijing Normal University.

Her area of research in the psychology and cognitive neuroscience in which she studies the psychological processes and neural mechanisms of emotion, human decision-making, and their interaction. She applies an interdisciplinary approach integrating cognitive neuroimaging with computational modeling, psychological measures, behavioral economics, and clinical disciplines. She published articles on journals like Hormones and Behavior, Scientific Reports, PlOS One, etc. She has been the reviewer of Journal of Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews, Neuropsychologia, PLOS One, Frontiers Neuroscience, Acta Psychologica Sinica, Artificial intelligence Review, etc. In 2018, She awarded overseas high-caliber personnel (peacock plan C talent) from the Shenzhen municipality and became working in Shenzhen. She is a backpacker, shutterbug, and writer in her spare time.
